Info about High School

My High school career has been rather hectic over the years. I have spent each and every year during high school in different locations. To summarize I started high school in Saudi Arabia, moved onto Italy for my sophomore year and finally enrolled to UCC for the final 2 years of my high school career. My first year in UCC was spent in Turkey where I did remote learning and my second was spent physically in Toronto. UCC is an IB school that seeks the utmost academic success of its students and I have been very content with the course of my learning.

Hobbies

I like to think of myself as a person who has multi-faceted interests. I am a sportive person who enjoys playing basketball in a competitive way while also engaging in activities such as saxophone playing and chess to develop my intellectual side

Aspirations

I hope to attend a prestigious university where I can also continue to puruse my extra-curricular passions. I would like to have the opportunity to play basketball in university and give myself the best chance at enhanching my post-university life in whichever carrer I may choose to pursue.
